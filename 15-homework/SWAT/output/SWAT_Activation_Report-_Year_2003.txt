 
SWAT Activations 2003- Spokane Police Department  
 
The SPD SWAT Team was activated a total of 30 times.  Below is a list and explanation of the 
number and types of SWAT activations.   
 
High Risk Warrant Service – 20 
These operations include warrant service for other SPD Units (SIU/Major Crimes/Regional Drug 
Unit, etc.) as well as outside agency requests (Washington State Patrol/Federal agencies). 
 
Tactical Operations – 9 
Tactical Operations include barricaded suspects, armed suicidal suspects, high-risk arrests and 
other high-risk operations (both ad hoc on patrol and prior planned).  
 
Special Events – 0 
 
Outside Agency Assists – 1
